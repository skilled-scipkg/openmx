# Visualization

The electron densities, molecular orbitals, and potentials are output
to files in the Gaussian cube format. Figure [13](visualization.md#fig:Vis) shows examples
of isosurface maps visualized by XCrySDen [[105](bibliography.md#XCrySDen)].
These data are output in the Gaussian cube format. So, many software,
such as VESTA [[103](bibliography.md#VESTA)], Molekel [[104](bibliography.md#Molekel)], and XCrySDen [[105](bibliography.md#XCrySDen)],
can be used for the visualization.
One can find the details of output files in the cube format in
the Section 'Output files'.
