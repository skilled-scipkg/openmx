# Molecular dynamics

OpenMX Ver. 3.9 supports five molecular dynamics simulations:
constant energy molecular dynamics (NVE),
constant temperature molecular dynamics by a velocity scaling (NVT_VS),
constant temperature molecular dynamics by a velocity scaling
to be considered independently for every atoms (NVT_VS2),
constant temperature molecular dynamics by the Nose-Hoover method (NVT_NH),
and
a multi-heat bath molecular dynamics (NVT_VS4).

Each of the molecular dynamics simulations will be explained in
the following subsections:
