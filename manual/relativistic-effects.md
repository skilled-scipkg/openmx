# Relativistic effects

Relativistic effects can be incorporated by fully relativistic
and scalar relativistic pseudopotentials.
In the fully relativistic treatment, the spin-orbit coupling is
included in addition to kinematic relativistic effects
(Darwin and mass velocity terms). On the other hand, the spin-orbit
coupling is averaged in the scalar relativistic treatment.
Although the scalar relativistic treatment can be incorporated
in both the collinear and non-collinear DFT calculations, the fully
relativistic treatment is supported for only the non-collinear DFT in OpenMX.
