## Examples

For user's convenience, input files for five examples can be found
in the directory 'work/cddf_examples' as follows:

- Febcc-Col_k30x30x30.dat
  Calculation of conductivities and dielectric functions for bcc iron with
  $30\times 30\times 30$ k-points.
- Mn12.dat
  Calculation of conductivities and dielectric functions for a Mn12 molecular magnet with
  $1\times 1\times 1$ k-points.
- Si2_k10x10x10.dat
  Calculation of conductivities and dielectric functions for a silicon bulk with
  $10\times 10\times 10$ k-points.
- Si2_k1xk1xk1.dat
  Calculation of conductivities and dielectric functions for a silicon bulk with
  $1\times 1\times 1$ k-points.
- VO2R-k16xk16x16.dat
  Calculation of conductivities and dielectric functions for VO2-R phase with
  $16\times 16\times 16$ k-points.
